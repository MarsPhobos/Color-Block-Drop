**Privacy Policy for Color Block Drop**

Last updated: January 2026

We do **not** collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or login credentials.

Color Block Drop uses the following third-party service:

**Google AdMob (Rewarded Ads)**
AdMob may collect and use information such as:

* Device information (model, OS version)
* Advertising ID
* Approximate location
* Ad interaction data

This data is used **only to show rewarded advertisements** and improve ad relevance.

Google’s Privacy Policy:
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 3. How We Use Information

Information collected by third-party services is used to:

* Display rewarded advertisements

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information.

---

### 4. Data Storage

Game progress such as:

* Level
* Bomb count
* Wind count

is stored **locally on your device** using Android SharedPreferences.
This data is **not transmitted to any server**.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.
